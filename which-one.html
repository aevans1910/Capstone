Which one to pick?

Sometimes it can be hard figuring out which type of animal is right for you. Although some individuals may have preferences, those may not be what is best depending on that individual's lifestyle. On this page, we will therefore discuss what type of individual is best suited to have a cat versus what individual should have a dog.

Activity

Depending on how active you are and whether or not you would like your companion to join you in that, a cat or a dog might be best.

If you are more active and want someone to join you, get a dog. If you are less active or don't want your furry companion joining you in your activities, get a cat.
